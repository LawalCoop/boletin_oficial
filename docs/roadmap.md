# Roadmap

## Working (MVP)

- Gazette ingestion — BORA, all 4 sections
- AI document transformation via Google Gemini
- Civic news portal with daily feed
- Filtering by jurisdiction, topic (22 temas), and date
- Full article view: summary, key points, affected groups, original text
- Contextual AI chat per article (Groq / Llama 3.3 70B)
- Admin panel for pipeline management
- Mobile-first responsive design
- User accounts with Google OAuth
- Voting system and saved articles
- 50+ real gazette articles processed

## Next

- Topic-based alerts (Telegram / WhatsApp) — meet communities where they already are
- Automated daily ingestion — from manual trigger to cron-based civic infrastructure
- "Legal diff" — what changed vs. previous law, who it affects
- Provincial gazette scrapers, starting with Neuquen
- RAG-powered cross-document search — "what changed about land rights this month?"
- Open data exports — structured gazette data as a public good

## Later

- Cumulative memory: timelines of how a regulation evolved over time
- Cross-jurisdiction comparison: detect contradictions between government levels
- Impact simulator inspired by OpenFisca
- WhatsApp alerts (phase 2 after Telegram)
- Municipal bulletin scrapers (VLA and other Patagonian towns)
